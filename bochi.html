Kessoku Band
Current Members of the Band

Hitori Gotoh - lead guitar, lyrics

Hitori Gotoh, also known as Bocchi, is a talented guitarist and songwriter in Kessoku Band.
She is shy, socially awkward, and prefers to express herself through music. Despite her stage fright,
her virtuoso guitar playing brings a unique sound to the band.

Nijika Ijichi - drums, leader

Nijika Ijichi is the energetic and passionate drummer of Kessoku Band. She is cheerful, outgoing, and serves as the driving force behind the group's formation. With her unwavering determination and love for music, Nijika keeps the band together both on and off stage, bringing rhythm and heart to every performance.

Ryo Yamada - bass

Ryo Yamada is the cool and enigmatic bassist of Kessoku Band. Known for her calm demeanor and quirky interests, she adds a unique edge to the group. Her solid basslines create a strong foundation for the band's sound, bringing depth and a subtle intensity to each song.

Ikuyo Kita - guitar, lead vocals

Ikuyo Kita is the vibrant vocalist and rhythm guitarist of Kessoku Band. Bright, cheerful, and full of determination, she brings charm and energy to the stage. Though she once struggled with confidence, Ikuyo now shines as a key performer, connecting with audiences through her heartfelt voice and passionate presence.
